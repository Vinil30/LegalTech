Title Deed Reference (Sale Deed/Gift Deed/Partition Deed/Will)

Names of the Parties Involved

Details about the individuals or entities involved in the deed.

  • Seller/Donor/Partitioner/Testator: Full name, address, and PAN details.
  • Buyer/Donee/Other Partitioner/Beneficiary: Full name, address, and PAN details.
Example: "This Sale Deed made and executed on this 15th day of October, 2024, by Mr. John Doe, residing at..."

Complete Property Description

A detailed description of the property being transferred.

  • Area: Specify the exact area (e.g., in square feet, acres).
  • Boundaries: North, South, East, and West boundaries clearly defined.
  • Survey Number: As per the land records.
  • Location: Village, Taluk, District, and State.

Example:

"All that piece and parcel of land bearing Survey No. 123/4, situated at Village ABC, Taluk XYZ, District PQR, State Tamil Nadu, measuring an extent of 2400 sq. ft. and bounded on the North by..., on the South by..., on the East by..., and on the West by..."

Consideration Amount / Value of Gift/Property

The monetary value exchanged in the transaction or the estimated value of the gift or property.

  • Sale Deed: The agreed-upon sale price.
  • Gift Deed: The estimated market value of the gifted property.
  • Partition Deed/Will: Value of the respective share
Example (Sale Deed): "The Sale Consideration for this transaction is Rs. 50,00,000 (Rupees Fifty Lakhs Only)."

Clear and Unambiguous Transfer of Ownership Clause

A statement explicitly transferring the ownership of the property.

  • Must clearly state the transfer of all rights, title, and interest.
Example: "The Seller hereby conveys, transfers, and assigns all his/her rights, title, and interest in the said property to the Buyer absolutely and forever."

Chain of Title (Previous Ownership History, if available)

A brief overview of the previous owners of the property.

  • Previous Owners Names and relevant document refrences
Example: "The said property was originally owned by Mr. XYZ, who sold it to Mr. ABC vide Sale Deed registered as Document No. 1234/2010..."

Registration Details

Information about the registration of the deed at the Sub-Registrar Office.

  • Book Number:
  • Volume Number:
  • Page Numbers:
  • Registration Date: Date of registration at the SRO.
  • Sub-Registrar Office Details: Name and address of the SRO.
Example: "Registered at the Sub-Registrar Office, Chennai South, as Document No. 5678/2024, in Book 1, Volume 234, Pages 123-130, dated 20th October 2024."

Signatures of all Parties and Witnesses

Signatures of the involved parties and witnesses.

  • Seller/Donor/Partitioner/Testator: Signature and full name.
  • Buyer/Donee/Other Partitioner/Beneficiary: Signature and full name.
  • Witnesses: At least two witnesses with signatures and full names.
Example: "Signed, Sealed, and Delivered by the within named Seller, Mr. John Doe, in the presence of:"

Stamp Duty Paid Details

Information regarding the stamp duty paid for the registration of the deed.

  • Amount:
  • Receipt Number:
  • Date of Payment:
Example: "Stamp duty of Rs. XX,XXX/- paid vide receipt no. ABC1234567, dated DD/MM/YYYY"

Mutation Records

Information about mutation in land records.

  • Mutation Order Number:
  • Date of Mutation:
  • Authority:
Example: "Mutation Order No. 1234/2024 dated DD/MM/YYYY issued by Talathi Office, ABC Village."

Date of Execution

The date on which the deed was executed (signed).

  • Date: The exact date on which the deed was signed by all parties.
Example: "This Sale Deed is executed on this 15th day of October, 2024."

Key Visual Features

Visual cues to identify a Title Deed.

  • Presence of revenue stamps or franking
  • Seal of the Sub-Registrar of Assurances
  • Notary Public seal and signature (if notarized)
  • Registration number and date
  • Typed or handwritten text (can be a mix)
  • Legal jargon and specific clauses

Layout Description

How the Title Deed usually looks.

Typically printed on stamp paper, with multiple pages. Each page will bear signatures and seals.

Typical Sections

  • Description of the Parties
  • Property Description
  • Consideration/Gift Clause
  • Transfer of Ownership Clause
  • Witness Attestation
  • Registration Details
  • Schedule of Property
